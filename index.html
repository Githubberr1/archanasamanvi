Musical Legacy

Hailing from the temple town of Udupi, Archana Upadhyaya and Samanvi embody the perfect blend of traditional Gurukula training and scholarly excellence[cite: 4, 6].

MSc Physics, Madras University

Archana Upadhyaya

An "A" grade artiste of AIR, Archana was initiated into music at a young age. Her style is marked by a powerful voice and deep raga exploration[cite: 16, 24]. She has been mentored by the Hebbar family and maestro Chitravina N. Ravikiran[cite: 6, 8].

MA Student, Madras University

Vidushi P. Samanvi

Samanvi is also a direct "A" grade AIR artiste. Known for her nimble voice and rhythmic precision, she shares a rare musical synchrony with Archana[cite: 5, 16, 63]. She is a recipient of the Mani Krishnaswamy Award and MS Subbulakshmi Voice of the Year[cite: 36, 47].
