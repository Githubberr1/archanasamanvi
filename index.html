Musical Legacy & The Journey

Hailing from the temple town of Udupi, the duo's music is a seamless blend of traditional rigor and captivating expression.

Vidushi Archana Upadhyay

Archana, known for her powerful voice and depth in *raga* exposition, was initiated into music at a tender age. She is a dedicated exponent of the *Gurukula* system, embodying the teachings of her revered Gurus. Her music reflects exceptional *Shruthi Shuddhate* (pitch accuracy) and a profound understanding of *bhava* (emotion).

Vidushi P. Samanvi

Samanvi complements Archana's style with her nimble voice and intricate *swara* patterns. She shares a complementary musical training, contributing to the duo's celebrated synchronization. Samanvi is a recipient of prestigious awards like the **Mani Krishnaswamy Annual Award**, marking her as a promising voice of the new generation.

Training & Mentorship

Their foundation was laid by **Smt. Radhika Shankar**. Their core training came under the affectionate tutelage of **Vid. Vasanthalaxmi Hebbar** and **Dr. V. Aravinda Hebbar**, who nurtured them in the *Gurukula Vasam* at 'Lathangi'. They receive advanced, scholarly guidance from the maestro **Chitravina N. Ravikiran** since 2015, shaping their nuanced musical presentations.
